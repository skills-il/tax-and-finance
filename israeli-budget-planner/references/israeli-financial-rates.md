## Mortgage (Mashkanta) Guidelines
### BOI Regulations
- Max LTV: 75% (first home), 70% (second home), 50% (investment)
- Max variable rate: 33.33% of total loan
- Max CPI-linked: 33.33% of total loan
- Minimum fixed component: 33.33%

### Typical Rate Ranges (2025)
| Track | Type | Range |
|-------|------|-------|
| Prime-linked | Variable | Prime +/- 0.5% |
| Fixed unlinked | Fixed | 4.5%-6.5% |
| CPI-linked fixed | Fixed + CPI | 3.0%-5.0% + CPI |
| CPI-linked variable | Resets 5yr | 2.5%-4.5% + CPI |
