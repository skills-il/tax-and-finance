# Israeli Tax-Deductible Expense Categories

Reference guide for categorizing business expenses according to Israeli tax law.
Source: Israel Tax Authority (Rashut HaMisim) guidelines for self-employed individuals.

## Fully Deductible Expenses (100%)

| Category EN | Category HE | Notes |
|-------------|-------------|-------|
| Office Rent | שכירות משרד | Lease agreements for dedicated business premises |
| Equipment | ציוד | Computers, monitors, office furniture (depreciable assets over 1,200 ILS) |
| Professional Services | שירותים מקצועיים | Accountant fees, legal counsel, consulting |
| Software & Subscriptions | תוכנה ומנויים | SaaS tools, cloud hosting, domain names |
| Marketing | שיווק | Advertising, website, business cards, trade shows |
| Insurance | ביטוח | Professional liability, business property insurance |
| Travel (Business) | נסיעות עסקיות | Flights, hotels, transport for business purposes |
| Training & Education | הכשרות והשתלמויות | Professional courses, conferences, certifications |
| Office Supplies | חומרי משרד | Paper, ink, stationery, cleaning supplies |
| Bank Fees | עמלות בנק | Business account maintenance fees, transaction fees |

## Partially Deductible Expenses

| Category EN | Category HE | Deduction Rate | Notes |
|-------------|-------------|----------------|-------|
| Meals & Entertainment | ארוחות ואירוח | 80% | Business meals with clients or partners |
| Car Expenses | הוצאות רכב | 45% or fixed | Fuel, maintenance, insurance, parking. Alternative: fixed deduction per km |
| Home Office | משרד ביתי | Proportional | Based on room area / total apartment area |
| Phone | טלפון | Proportional | Business-use portion only. If separate business line, 100% |
| Internet | אינטרנט | Proportional | Business-use portion. If business-only connection, 100% |

## Non-Deductible Expenses

| Category EN | Category HE | Notes |
|-------------|-------------|-------|
| Personal Expenses | הוצאות אישיות | Clothing (unless uniforms), personal meals |
| Fines & Penalties | קנסות | Traffic fines, tax penalties |
| Gifts over 220 ILS | מתנות מעל 220 ש"ח | Per recipient per year |
| Entertainment without business purpose | בילוי ללא מטרה עסקית | Must document business connection |

## VAT (Ma'am) Rules

- Standard VAT rate: **17%** (since 2016)
- VAT registration threshold: Revenue over 107,692 ILS/year (2024, updated annually)
- Below threshold: "Patur" (exempt dealer), no VAT charged or reclaimed
- Above threshold: "Osek Murshe" (authorized dealer), must charge and report VAT
- Reporting frequency: Bi-monthly (every 2 months) for most businesses
- Large businesses (revenue over ~2.5M ILS): Monthly VAT reporting
