# Israeli Tax Withholding Rates Reference

## Default Rates by Payment Type

### Section 164 — Income Payments
| Payment Type | Rate | Notes |
|-------------|------|-------|
| Services (individual) | 20% | Consulting, freelance work |
| Services (company) | 20-30% | Varies by tax office classification |
| Interest | 25% | Bank and non-bank interest |
| Dividends | 25% | Standard shareholders |
| Dividends (major shareholder) | 30% | Shareholders holding > 10% |
| Prizes and awards | 25% | Competition winnings, etc. |

### Section 170 — Special Payments
| Payment Type | Rate | Notes |
|-------------|------|-------|
| Rent (business property) | 35% | Commercial real estate |
| Royalties | 23% | Intellectual property payments |
| Payments to non-residents | 25% | Subject to tax treaty reduction |
| Insurance commissions | 20% | Agent commissions |
| Construction contractors | 20% | Building and renovation |

## Tax Treaty Reduced Rates (Common)
| Country | Dividends | Interest | Royalties |
|---------|-----------|----------|-----------|
| USA | 12.5-25% | 17.5% | 15% |
| UK | 15% | 15% | 15% |
| Germany | 10-25% | 15% | 5% |
| France | 10-15% | 10% | 10% |
| Canada | 15% | 15% | 15% |

NOTE: Treaty rates require proper documentation and forms.
Always verify specific treaty provisions.

## Withholding Certificate Rates
Businesses can apply for reduced rates:
- 0% — Clean tax record, established business
- 2% — Most common reduced rate
- 5% — Standard reduced rate
- 10% — Moderate reduction
- Custom rate based on tax assessment
