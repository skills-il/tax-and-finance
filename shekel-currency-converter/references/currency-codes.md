## Tax-Relevant Uses
- **VAT on imports:** Convert foreign currency to NIS at representative rate on transaction date
- **Foreign income:** Report at representative rate on income date
- **Foreign expenses:** Deduct at representative rate on payment date
- **End-of-year revaluation:** Use December 31 rate for balance sheet items

## NIS Symbol and Formatting
- Currency code: ILS (ISO 4217)
- Symbol: (shekel sign, Unicode U+20AA)
- Common display: NIS or ILS
- Format: 1,234.56 NIS (thousands separator: comma, decimal: period)
- Hebrew format: 1,234.56 (symbol before number)
